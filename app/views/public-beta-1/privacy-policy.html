Privacy notice

The Ministry of Justice (MoJ) is committed to the protection and security of your personal information.

This notice describes how and why we are using such information.

This privacy notice describes how we collect and use personal information during and after your relationship with us, in accordance with data protection law. This notice will be updated regularly.

The type of personal data we process

We collect and process information related to child arrangement planning to help you identify child arrangement planning options suitable for your circumstances. However, we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, addresses, or contact details.

Because we are not gathering information that can be used to identify you, many data protection rights do not apply.

The types of information that we collect which are not personally identifiable may include:

  • user interactions with our platform, including navigation behaviour and feature usage, to improve our service

We do not share the answers you provide with any other organisation or government department.
