The MoJ accepts no liability for:

  • you being unable to access the service (for example due to high demand or because we've taken it down for maintenance)

  • the content and availability of external websites that link to the service or which link from it

Information about you and your site visits

We collect and process information related to:

  • child arrangement planning to help you identify child arrangement planning options suitable for your circumstances
  • how you use the service to help make sure it is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements

By using this service you agree to us collecting this information.

However, we do not collect personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, addresses, or contact details, in accordance with our privacy notice and cookie policy.

Limitations of the service

Using this service does not guarantee that you will be able to resolve your child arrangements.

Misuse

You must not impersonate another person, provide false or misleading information, or use this service on behalf of someone else without their consent.

Restrictions on use

If you have a court order in place that restricts your contact with the other parent or child, such as a no contact order, non-molestation order, or any other legal restriction, you must not use this service to contact them.

Doing so could be a breach of the order and may have legal consequences. If you are unsure whether a court order or other legal restriction affects your ability to use this service, seek legal advice before proceeding.
