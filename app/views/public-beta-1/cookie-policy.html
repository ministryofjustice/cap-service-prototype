Cookies

The Get help finding a child arrangement service puts small files (known as 'cookies') onto your computer to collect information about how you browse the service.

Cookies are used to:

  • remember your progress
  • measure how you use the service so it can be updated and improved based on your needs

Get help finding a child arrangement cookies aren't used to identify you personally.

You'll normally see a message before we store a cookie on your computer.

Find out more about how to manage cookies

How cookies are used in this service

Essential cookies

Essential cookies are needed for the service to work. We do not need to ask for permission to use them.

Remembering your progress

We will store cookies to remember your application progress in this computer and to expire your session after 120 minutes of inactivity or when you close your browser.

Name Purpose Expires
pfl-connected-services.session Saves your current progress in this computer and tracks inactivity periods After 120 minutes of inactivity or when you close your browser

Cookies message

You may see a banner when you visit Get help finding a child arrangement option inviting you to accept or reject optional cookies.

We'll set cookies so that your computer knows you've seen it and not to show it again, and also to store your settings.

Name Purpose Expires
cookie_setting Saves your cookie consent settings 1 year

Analytics cookies (optional)

Measuring service usage (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics software to collect information about how you use Get help finding a child arrangement option. We do this to help make sure the service is meeting the needs of its users and to help us make improvements.

Google Analytics stores information about:

  • the pages you visit
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the service
  • what you click on while you're visiting the service

We don't collect or store your personal information (for example your name or address) so this information can't be used to identify who you are.

Google Analytics sets the following cookies:

Universal analytics

Name Purpose Expires
_ga This helps us count how many people visit Get help finding a child arrangement option by tracking if you've visited before 2 years
_ga_YQSM58BJ59 Used by Google Analytics to find and track an individual session with your device 2 years
