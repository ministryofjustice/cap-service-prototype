Which schedule best meets the children's needs?

{% set schedulingIdeasHtml %}

Here are some common schedules that can work well for children.

Alternating weeks

The children will spend one week in one household and the next week in the other.

2-2-3 schedule

Children spend two days in one household, two days in the other, then back to the first house for 3 days including the weekend.

4-4-3 schedule

Children spend three days in one household then four days in the other. The next week they switch.

2-2-5-5 schedule

Children spend two days in one household, then two days in the other. After that they spend five days in one household, then five days in the other.
